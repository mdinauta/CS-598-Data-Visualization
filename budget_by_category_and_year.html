COVID-19 Impact to the Federal Budget

Federal Spending by Category and Year

This chart displays federal spending by both category and year.
We see that the large categories of Social Security, Medicare, and Medicade increased in 2020 by a typical amount, while other categories increased much more than usual. This again is reflecting spending related to COVID-19.

Hover the mouse over a category in the legend to display the value for that category.
Click-and-drag over the chart area to zoom in on a time period.
